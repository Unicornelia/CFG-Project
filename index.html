Dance

From Ballet to Breakdance, all types of dance keeps both the body and the brain active, vital for people of every age.
Dance offers a way to improve strength and flexibility, which helps keeps muscles and joints healthy.
Join us as CSN takes you through various styles of dance while you better your fitness and fill yourself with vitality.

Yoga

Psychology

Taking care of our psyche is just as important as to care for our body.

Our holistic centre believes that everyone can experience times in their lives that are hard to deal with and a little extra help could move you from that tipping point.

If you are interested please contact one of our consultants for private counselling sessions.

We follow a holistic approach in councelling too, combining traditional and modern techniques, focusing on the whole person, rather than just one single problem.
